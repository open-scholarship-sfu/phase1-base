# Code of Conduct

### Open Scholarship Framework Initiative

The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to support a community where all people should feel safe to participate, introduce new ideas and inspire others, and applies to all participants of the Open Scholarship Framework Initiative - Simon Fraser University. This Code aims to promote a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment that supports the mission of the Initiative, within or beyond the limits of the SFU *campi*.

## Principles

1. **Respect**: All members of the OSFI community are expected to treat each other with respect and dignity, regardless of differences in background, beliefs, or opinions.

2. **Integrity**: Members of the OSFI community should act with honesty and integrity in all academic and professional activities.

3. **Inclusivity**: OSFI is committed to fostering an inclusive environment where diversity is valued and all individuals feel welcome and supported.

4. **Safety**: The safety and well-being of all members of the OSFI community is a priority. Harassment, discrimination, and violence will not be tolerated.

5. **Accountability**: Members of the OSFI community are responsible for their actions and must adhere to this Code of Conduct.

## Desired behaviours

- Treat others with respect and kindness.
- Listen actively and consider different perspectives.
- Communicate openly and honestly.
- Support a diverse and inclusive environment.
- Report any violations of this Code of Conduct to the appropriate authorities.
- Participate in creating a positive and safe community.
- Engage in constructive dialogue and collaboration.
- Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on others.
